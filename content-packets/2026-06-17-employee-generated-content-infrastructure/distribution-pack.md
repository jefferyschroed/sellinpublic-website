## LinkedIn Post Option 1

Employee-generated content is not the same as employee advocacy.

Advocacy usually distributes company-approved content through employee accounts.

Employee-generated content starts with the employee's own knowledge: a customer pattern, workflow, build note, teardown, tutorial, or decision.

The useful test is simple: does the piece teach something from real work, or does it repeat the announcement?

Full guide: https://sellinpublic.co/blog/employee-generated-content-infrastructure/

## LinkedIn Post Option 2

Some employees should post publicly. Others should contribute notes, examples, customer questions, or technical explanations that an editor can shape.

The goal is not to make every employee a creator.

The goal is to turn the knowledge already inside the company into public learning that buyers and practitioners can use.

Guide and checklist: https://sellinpublic.co/blog/employee-generated-content-infrastructure/
