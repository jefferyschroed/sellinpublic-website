## Search Promise

Answer what employee-generated content is, how it differs from employee advocacy, what useful B2B examples look like, and how teams can turn employee expertise into public learning without making every employee a creator.

## Answer-First Summary

Employee-generated content is original public content based on what employees know, see, build, test, sell, support, or learn. It is useful when it teaches from a real employee vantage point instead of repackaging a company announcement.
