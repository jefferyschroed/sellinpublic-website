# Distribution Pack

## LinkedIn Post Option

Most teams ask for employee-generated content examples and get a list of formats.

The better question is: what work became visible?

Clay shows decision logic. Lovable shows founder and product teaching. GitLab shows durable operating knowledge. Shopify Engineering shows technical judgment.

The format matters less than the source of insight.

## Email Teaser

Subject: What good employee-generated content examples have in common

Good examples do not start with a content calendar. They start with work worth explaining. This post breaks down examples from Clay, Lovable, GitLab, and Shopify Engineering.

## Outreach Angle

Use when a prospect says the team does not know what employees should post. Route them to the checklist and ask which internal workflows already create buyer-useful lessons.
