# Distribution Pack

## LinkedIn Post Option

Most teams ask for employee-generated content examples and get a list of formats.

The better question is: what actual public asset can I inspect?

Clay shows founder narrative around GTM category work. Lovable shows founder-led market narrative. GitLab shows durable operating knowledge. Shopify Engineering shows technical judgment.

The format matters less than the source of insight, but the URL matters. If the reader cannot inspect it, it is not a useful examples page.

## Email Teaser

Subject: What good employee-generated content examples have in common

Good examples do not start with a content calendar. They start with work worth explaining. This post breaks down public posts and source assets from Clay, Lovable, GitLab, and Shopify Engineering.

## Outreach Angle

Use when a prospect says the team does not know what employees should post. Route them to the checklist and ask which internal workflows already create buyer-useful lessons.
