## Usable Insights

- A good employee-generated content example starts with a person or team inside the company seeing something clearly enough to teach it.
- The public artifact can be a LinkedIn post, founder note, engineering article, support breakdown, handbook page, webinar clip, or sales field note.
- The operating test is not "did an employee share it?" The test is whether the piece contains expertise that only someone close to the work could explain.
- Examples should be judged by buyer usefulness, sales reuse, internal learning, search visibility, and follow-up questions, not by impressions alone.
