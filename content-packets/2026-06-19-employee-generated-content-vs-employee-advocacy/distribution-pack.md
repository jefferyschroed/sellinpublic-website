## LinkedIn Post Angle

Employee-generated content and employee advocacy are often treated like the same thing. They are not.

Employee advocacy helps a company-approved message travel.

Employee-generated content starts with what employees know from real work: customer questions, product lessons, sales objections, implementation patterns, founder POV, and technical tradeoffs.

The difference matters because the workflow and metrics are different.

If the goal is reach for a launch, advocacy fits.

If the goal is useful expertise that buyers can inspect, employee-generated content fits.

If the goal is a launch plus credible field context, use both.
