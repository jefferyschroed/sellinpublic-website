## LinkedIn Post Option

Founder content and employee content do different jobs.

Founder-led content sets the company's point of view.

Employee-generated content proves how the team thinks through real work.

Most B2B teams do not need to choose one forever. They need to decide which job LinkedIn needs to do this quarter: set the market stance, show role-level expertise, or connect both.

Full guide: https://sellinpublic.co/blog/founder-content-vs-employee-content/

## Email Teaser

Subject: Founder content or employee content?

The short answer: use founder-led content when the market needs a stance. Use employee-generated content when buyers need proof from people doing the work. Use both when direction and proof need to travel together.
