# Founder Content Vs Employee Content

## Search Promise

Answer when a B2B company should use founder-led LinkedIn content, employee-generated content, or both.

## Answer-First Summary

Use founder-led content when the company needs a market stance or executive narrative. Use employee-generated content when buyers need role-level proof from people doing the work. Use both when the founder can set direction and employees can make the company's expertise inspectable.
