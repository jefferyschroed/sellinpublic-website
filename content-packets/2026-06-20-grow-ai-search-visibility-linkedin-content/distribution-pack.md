# Distribution Pack

## LinkedIn Post Option 1

AI search visibility is not a LinkedIn-only problem.

LinkedIn can help because real employee expertise is visible there. But the controlled answer should still live on a crawlable owned page with sources, structure, and internal links.

The stronger loop:

1. Pull buyer questions from sales and support.
2. Let the right employee answer one question on LinkedIn.
3. Expand the strongest answer into an owned blog page.
4. Link the post and page together.
5. Track social response, search visibility, and AI citations separately.

Feed posts create signals. Owned pages hold the answer.

## LinkedIn Post Option 2

Posting more on LinkedIn is not an AI search strategy.

A better system is narrower:

- Employees answer real buyer questions.
- The best answers become structured blog pages.
- Blog pages carry the sources, examples, schema, and internal links.
- LinkedIn keeps the expertise current and visible.

Use LinkedIn for expert signal. Use owned pages for the durable answer.

## Email Teaser

Subject: LinkedIn helps AI visibility, but it is not the whole system

This guide explains how to connect LinkedIn posts to owned answer pages so your team has both public expert signals and crawlable source material.
