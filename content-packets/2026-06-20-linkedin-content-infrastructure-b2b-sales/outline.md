## Answer-First Summary

B2B sales teams should build LinkedIn content infrastructure by turning buyer questions and sales insight into a weekly operating loop. The loop needs source capture, credible team voices, profile standards, an editorial calendar, fast review, a publishing and commenting rhythm, measurement beyond impressions, and a repurposing path into owned content and outbound.
