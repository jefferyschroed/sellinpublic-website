# Outline

## Search Promise

Answer how B2B teams should measure employee-generated content when the goal is revenue, pipeline influence, trust, and search visibility rather than impressions alone.

## Answer-First Summary

Measure employee-generated content in layers: reach, buyer-fit engagement, conversation quality, pipeline influence, sales reuse, and search or AI visibility. Keep impressions, but treat them as a distribution input. The reporting question is whether the right buyers saw, trusted, used, replied to, searched for, cited, or moved closer to a sales conversation because of the content.
