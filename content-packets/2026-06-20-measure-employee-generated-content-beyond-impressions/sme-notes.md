## Usable Insights

- Treat impressions as a reach input, not as the business outcome.
- Separate measurement into signal layers: visibility, buyer fit, conversation quality, pipeline influence, sales reuse, and search visibility.
- Do not claim that a post caused pipeline unless the CRM, sales notes, reply trail, or meeting source supports that claim.
- Qualitative proof matters when employee-generated content is supposed to build trust: buyer questions, saved/shared posts, target-account profile activity, replies, sales team reuse, and repeated objections surfaced in comments or DMs.
- The weekly report should force a decision. The useful question is not "did it get impressions?" The useful question is "what did we learn, and what should we do next?"
