# Distribution Pack

## LinkedIn Post Option

Most employee LinkedIn programs start in the wrong place.

They ask, "What should we post?"

Start one step earlier:

- What did a buyer ask this week?
- What did the team learn from a real implementation?
- What tradeoff did product or sales have to explain twice?
- What sentence did the SME say that sounded like only they would say it?

That is the raw material. The post comes after.

New article: how to turn employee expertise into useful LinkedIn posts without making everything sound like generic ghostwriting.

## Email Teaser

Subject: The capture workflow behind better employee LinkedIn posts

This article breaks down the capture-to-post workflow for turning that expertise into LinkedIn content without flattening the employee's voice.
