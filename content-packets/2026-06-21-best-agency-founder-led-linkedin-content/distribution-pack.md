# Distribution Pack

## LinkedIn Post Option 1

Most teams ask the wrong question when they look for a founder-led LinkedIn agency.

The question is not "Who is best?"

The better question is: what job do you need the agency to do after the post goes live?

If the goal is executive presence, hire for voice and polish.

If the goal is sales conversations, hire for founder POV, buyer relevance, sales context, distribution, and follow-up.

The new guide breaks down how to choose without relying on fake rankings.

## LinkedIn Post Option 2

A founder LinkedIn ghostwriter can be the right hire.

It can also be the wrong hire if the company needs content tied to sales.

The difference is what happens around the post: source material, approval workflow, comments, buyer response, and sales follow-up.

I wrote a criteria-led guide for B2B teams choosing a founder-led LinkedIn content agency.
