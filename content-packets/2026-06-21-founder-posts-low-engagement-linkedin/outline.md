# Outline

## Search Promise

Answer why founder LinkedIn posts get low engagement, then give a practical diagnostic that a B2B revenue team can run before asking the founder to post more often.

## Answer-First Summary

Founder posts usually get low engagement because they are written for the company instead of the buyer. The common causes are a weak first line, company news with no buyer stakes, no founder-owned point of view, a thin comment plan, no employee distribution loop, weak profile context, inconsistent cadence, and metrics that reward surface attention instead of buyer relevance.
