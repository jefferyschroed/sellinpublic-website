# Outline

## Search Promise

Answer what LinkedIn employee advocacy is, how a B2B revenue team should run it, and how to keep employee voices from becoming generic brand amplification.

## Answer-First Summary

LinkedIn employee advocacy is coordinated employee participation around company or team messages on LinkedIn. A revenue team should run it as a voluntary weekly loop: choose a buyer-relevant moment, identify the employees with standing to participate, give them context and optional prompts, let them personalize the action, and measure buyer signals after the post moves.
