# Distribution Pack

## LinkedIn Post Option 1

Most employee-generated content programs stall because the team asks for posts before it builds the support system.

Start smaller:

- 5 to 8 credible employees
- 1 weekly expertise capture loop
- 3 to 5 profile-native drafts
- 1 light review lane
- 1 sales follow-up review

The point is not to make every employee a creator. The point is to turn the team's buyer-facing knowledge into posts that sound like the people who earned that knowledge.

## LinkedIn Post Option 2

Employee advocacy and employee-generated content should not be managed as the same motion.

Advocacy starts with a company message and asks employees to help it travel.

Employee-generated content starts with what an employee knows from doing the work, then gives that person support to publish in their own voice.

That difference changes the system: interviews, profile-native drafts, light review, and sales follow-up matter more than company-wide reshare asks.
