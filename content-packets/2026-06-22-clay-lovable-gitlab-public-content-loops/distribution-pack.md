## LinkedIn Post Option 2

A strong public content loop needs three parts:

- Real work from the team.
- A public artifact buyers can inspect.
- A route for updates, questions, reuse, or contribution.

Clay does this with workflows.

Lovable does this with builders and docs.

GitLab does this with the handbook.

Most B2B teams can start smaller: one useful buyer-facing field note per week, then let replies and sales conversations shape the next one.
