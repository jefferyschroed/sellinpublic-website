## Usable Insights

- A public content loop needs a real work source, a public artifact, and a feedback route.
- Clay is useful because the artifact is often a reusable workflow, not only a story about the company.
- Lovable is useful because the public surface includes builders, launch activity, remix behavior, docs, and public correction when visibility rules changed.
- GitLab is useful because the public handbook turns internal operating knowledge into a durable reference.
- Revenue teams should not copy the surface format. They should identify the real work they can make inspectable.
