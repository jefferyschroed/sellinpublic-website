# Distribution Pack

Packet: `2026-06-22-review-employee-generated-content-without-killing-voice`

## LinkedIn Option 1

Most employee content review fails because every reviewer edits like the final writer.

Run the review in two passes:

1. Risk: facts, confidentiality, disclosure, legal-sensitive claims, regulated routing, and platform policy.
2. Voice: clarity without removing the employee's judgment.

Every requested change should name the reason. If the reason is only that the reviewer would have written it differently, leave the sentence alone.

## LinkedIn Option 2

Employee-generated content needs review. It does not need five people rewriting the post into one brand voice.

The practical fix is a change-type system:

- Fact
- Confidentiality
- Disclosure
- Legal routing
- Regulated routing
- Brand boundary
- Clarity
- Voice

That small label keeps the reviewer focused on the job the edit is doing.
