## Search Promise

Give B2B revenue teams a practical review workflow for employee-generated content that protects accuracy, confidentiality, disclosure, brand boundaries, and regulated review needs without making every employee sound like the same brand account.

## Answer-First Summary

Teams should review employee-generated content in two separate passes. First, check facts, disclosure, confidentiality, customer approval, legal-sensitive claims, regulated-industry rules, and platform policy risk. Second, edit for clarity while preserving the employee's judgment, specific language, and point of view. Review comments should name the reason for each change. If the reason is only that a reviewer would have written it differently, leave the employee's voice alone.
