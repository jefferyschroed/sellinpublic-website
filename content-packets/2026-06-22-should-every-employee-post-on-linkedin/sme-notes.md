## Usable Insights

- Every employee does not need to publish original LinkedIn posts.
- The right operating model is role-based participation: some employees publish, some comment or send privately, and some sit out.
- Mandatory posting quotas create voice, trust, review, and morale problems.
- Employee-generated content should start with firsthand expertise, not a company-wide request to amplify brand copy.
- Employee advocacy can still matter when it gives employees context, options, and voluntary ways to participate.
