# Outline

## Search Promise

Teach a privacy-safe workflow for turning sales-call questions, objections, proof requests, and explanations into employee-generated LinkedIn post ideas.

## Answer-First Summary

Sales calls become useful employee content when the team captures patterns, not private details. Log the buyer question, objection, phrase, proof request, or explanation moment. Strip customer identifiers and sensitive deal context. Check recording consent, retention, access, and company policy. Then route the abstracted idea to the employee with standing to explain it.

### Use CRM notes without publishing CRM details

- HubSpot and Microsoft show call records can include transcripts, summaries, associated records, keywords, notes, and action items.
- Use those fields to find themes, not to publish private context.
