# Foundation 20-Post Publishing Plan

Updated: 2026-06-19

This is the tracked publishing plan for the foundation push. It turns the 20 foundation opportunities into publishable article candidates without authorizing a 20-post mass publish.

## Current Production Rule

Post #1, `employee-generated-content-vs-employee-advocacy`, was produced for owner review and used to capture the first revision rules for source integration and brand-neutral informational copy.

Post #2, `employee-generated-content-infrastructure`, has now been refreshed as the next owner-review post. Posts #3-#20 stay in planning until post #2 is reviewed and approved by the owner. After each manual post, the next article needs owner revisions or approval before packet work, generation, or publishing.

Use this sequence as the working backlog for packets, source acquisition, SME notes, internal links, and approval routing. Do not create blog posts directly from this file.

## Gates

Each article needs:

- Source readiness checked or refreshed.
- Query handoff reviewed.
- SME or operator notes where public sources are not enough.
- Parent, pillar, cannibalization, and internal-link role confirmed.
- Packet intake approved before outline or draft work.
- Publish governor approval before generated blog output.
