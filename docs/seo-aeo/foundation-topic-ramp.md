# Foundation Topic Ramp

Updated: 2026-06-18

This doc preserves the operating decision for the first broad topic push: build 20 topic foundations and packet opportunities, not 20 immediate published posts.

## Rule

Do not mass-publish 20 posts from discovery data.

Use the 20-topic foundation as a backlog for topical authority, source acquisition, AI citation monitoring, internal-link planning, and packet intake. A topic becomes a published post only after it clears source readiness, SME needs, query handoff, packet QA, the publish governor, and owner approval.

## Why

Discovery clusters are useful for coverage planning, but they are not evidence. Publishing many pages before source and performance signals exist would make the learning loop noisy: weak rankings, citation gaps, and low engagement would be hard to attribute to topic choice, source quality, structure, internal links, or page format.

The first ramp should increase learning quality, not content volume.

## Gate Mapping

- `80+` score: standalone packet candidate after source and handoff gates.
- `65-79` score: resolve source, SME, example, or POV gap before packet work.
- `50-64` score: use as H2, FAQ, table row, checklist item, or refresh note.
- `<50` score: park, merge, or retire unless it fills a known coverage gap.

## Dispatch Rules

- One subagent owns one next action.
- Topic Cartographer maps parent, pillar, cannibalization risk, and internal-link role.
- Query Intelligence validates demand and AEO prompt language.
- Source Registry verifies factual support and source freshness.
- SME Notes adds operator context where public sources are insufficient.
- Outline and Draft work only after packet intake is clean.
- Claim Ledger and QA agents cannot approve their own prose.
- Publish Governor decides whether a ready packet can be generated.

## Cadence Position

Today: build the 20-topic foundation, finish AI citation baseline checks, and keep publishing blocked unless the governor selects a ready packet.

Default next cadence: one strong post per week after gates clear. Increase only after indexing, GA4, Search Console, AI citation, source QA, and content-decision evidence become stable enough to learn from.
