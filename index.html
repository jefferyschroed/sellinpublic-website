Frequently asked questions

The questions teams ask before turning authority into pipeline.

Sell In Public is built for operators who want a managed revenue system, not another disconnected content calendar or outbound tool.

Who writes the content?

Sell In Public leads strategy, interviews subject matter experts, mines sales calls and customer proof, then drafts posts in the executive or employee voice.

Do you manage employee LinkedIn accounts?

Yes. We can operate the weekly LinkedIn queue across executive content, employee-generated content, profile touches, comments, connections, DMs, and follow-up.

How do cold DMs and email work together?

Both channels run from the same ICP, account triggers, and offer logic. LinkedIn creates visibility and context; email adds reach and sequencing.

Can you work with our SDRs and AEs?

Yes. SDRs and AEs receive account context, reply summaries, suggested next steps, content proof, and warm handoffs.

How fast can we launch?

Most teams can move from discovery to a live operating cadence in one to two weeks when ICP, offer, access, and approvals are clear.

What do you need from our team?

We need a clear owner, buyer insight, approved proof, account priorities, current positioning, and a review cadence.

Is this a fit if we already have an agency?

Usually, yes. Sell In Public owns the GTM operating cadence across voice, distribution, outbound, reply handling, and weekly iteration.

How do you report performance?

Reporting centers on sales movement: posts shipped, accounts touched, connection accepts, engagement, positive replies, booked conversations, and message learnings.
