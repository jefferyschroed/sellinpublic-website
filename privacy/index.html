Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Sell In Public collects, how we use it, and how to contact us about privacy requests.

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Information we collect

We collect information you choose to provide when you contact us, book a call, subscribe to updates, or work with us. This may include your name, email address, company, role, LinkedIn profile, message content, and details about your go-to-market needs.

We may also collect basic website analytics such as pages visited, referring pages, device and browser information, and approximate location derived from standard log data. These analytics help us understand site performance and improve our content.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, schedule and prepare for calls, provide services, operate the website, improve our content and offers, protect the site from abuse, and maintain business records.

Service providers

We may use service providers for website hosting, analytics, scheduling, email, CRM, payment, document, and communication workflows. These providers process information for the business purposes described in this policy.

Cookies and analytics

The site may use cookies or similar technologies for analytics and performance measurement. You can accept or decline non-essential analytics and advertising cookies through the cookie banner or Cookie settings control, and you can also control cookies through your browser settings.

Advertising and identity-resolution partners

When you visit or log in to our website, we and our online data partners or vendors may use cookies, scripts, pixels, and similar technologies to connect website activity with other personal information they or others have about you, including an email address. We, or service providers acting for us, may then use that information to send communications and marketing to those email addresses.

You may opt out of this advertising by visiting https://app.retention.com/optout. If GDPR applies to you, you may also opt out of RB2B personal data collection by visiting https://www.rb2b.com/rb2b-gdpr-opt-out.

Data sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, when needed to provide services, to comply with legal obligations, to protect rights and security, or in connection with a business transfer.

Your choices

You can ask us to update, delete, or provide a copy of personal information you have shared with us, subject to legal and operational requirements. You can also unsubscribe from non-transactional emails by using the unsubscribe option where provided or by contacting us.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email jeff@sellinpublic.co.
